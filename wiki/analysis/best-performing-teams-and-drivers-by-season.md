#### Key Driver Highlights (2026):
1. **Kimi Antonelli ([[drivers/kimi-antonelli]])**: Established one of the most dominant individual starts to a debut season in F1 history, claiming 6 victories out of 10 races (China, Japan, Miami, Canada, Monaco, Belgium), 8 pole positions, and 6 DHL Fastest Laps (source: raw/Kimi Antonelli - F1 Driver for Mercedes.md).
2. **Lewis Hamilton ([[drivers/lewis-hamilton]])**: In his historic first season with [[teams/ferrari]], Hamilton achieved victory at the 2026 Spanish Grand Prix in Barcelona and secured 5 podiums, holding 2nd in the Drivers' Championship with 159 points (source: raw/Lewis Hamilton - F1 Driver for Ferrari.md).
3. **George Russell ([[drivers/george-russell]])**: Claimed 2 victories (Australia and Austria) and 5 podiums, forming the grid's top driver pairing alongside Antonelli at [[teams/mercedes]].

#### Key Team Highlights (2026):
1. **Mercedes ([[teams/mercedes]])**: Won 8 out of 10 Grands Prix (80% win rate) and secured all 10 pole positions (100% pole rate), scoring 358 points behind Antonelli and Russell.
2. **Ferrari ([[teams/ferrari]])**: Secured 2 wins (Spain and Great Britain) and 9 podium finishes, accumulating 285 points with Hamilton and Leclerc.
3. **McLaren ([[teams/mclaren]])**: Ranked 3rd with 195 points and 4 podiums, leveraging strong driver parity between Norris (103 pts) and Piastri (92 pts).
4. **Midfield Battle (Alpine vs Racing Bulls)**: Tied at 61 points in 5th place, with Alpine backed by Gasly's Monaco podium and Racing Bulls powered by Lawson's 100% race finish record.
