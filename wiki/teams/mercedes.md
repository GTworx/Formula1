## Detailed Breakdown

### 2026 Season Performance
In the 2026 FIA Formula 1 World Championship, Mercedes currently sits in 1st position in the Constructors' Standings with 358 points (source: raw/Mercedes – F1 Racing Team.md). Driven by [[drivers/kimi-antonelli]], [[drivers/george-russell]], the team has collected 8 victories, 13 podium finishes, and 15 top-10 finishes across the opening 10 rounds of the 22-race calendar.

### Team Profile & Technical Structure
Operating from its base in Brackley, United Kingdom, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team competes under the leadership of Toto Wolff with technical guidance from James Allison. The team's 2026 entry features the W17 chassis powered by Mercedes engines (source: raw/Mercedes – F1 Racing Team.md).
