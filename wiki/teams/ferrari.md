## Detailed Breakdown

### 2026 Season Performance
In the 2026 FIA Formula 1 World Championship, Ferrari currently sits in 2nd position in the Constructors' Standings with 285 points (source: raw/Ferrari – F1 Racing Team.md). Driven by [[drivers/lewis-hamilton]], [[drivers/charles-leclerc]], the team has collected 2 victories, 9 podium finishes, and 18 top-10 finishes across the opening 10 rounds of the 22-race calendar.

### Team Profile & Technical Structure
Operating from its base in Maranello, Italy, Scuderia Ferrari HP competes under the leadership of Frédéric Vasseur with technical guidance from Loic Serra. The team's 2026 entry features the SF-26 chassis powered by Ferrari engines (source: raw/Ferrari – F1 Racing Team.md).
