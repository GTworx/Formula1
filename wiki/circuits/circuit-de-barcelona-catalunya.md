### Track Profile & 2026 Race Summary
Located in Montmeló, Spain, Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya hosted Round 7 of the 2026 Formula 1 World Championship, the [[races/2026-spanish-gp]] (source: raw/F1 - The Official Home of Formula 1® Racing 5.md).
