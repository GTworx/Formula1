### Track Profile & 2026 Race Summary
Located in Suzuka, Japan, Suzuka Circuit hosted Round 3 of the 2026 Formula 1 World Championship, the [[races/2026-japanese-gp]] (source: raw/F1 - The Official Home of Formula 1® Racing 5.md).

The 2026 race was won by [[drivers/kimi-antonelli]] driving for [[teams/mercedes]], finishing ahead of [[drivers/oscar-piastri]] and [[drivers/charles-leclerc]]. The fastest lap of the weekend was clocked by [[drivers/kimi-antonelli]] at 1:32.432 (source: raw/F1 - The Official Home of Formula 1® Racing 3.md).
